# Classic models

### Principal insights obtained from data analysed:
  1. Classic cars were the most product type sold between 2003-2005.
  2. The most selling model was '1992 Ferrari 360 Spider red' with 1720 units shipped around the world.
  3. Euro+ Shopping Channel was the company with more units acquired during these years; in consequence, the company with more amount invested in these products.
  4. Gerard Hernandez is the most succesful sales rep with more than 12K units sold and shipped.
  5. USA is the country with more units ordered between 2003-2005.
